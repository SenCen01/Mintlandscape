Our Mission

Shaping BC one
property at a time.

Mint Landscape was founded on a straightforward idea: that every outdoor space deserves to be treated with the same care and skill as the people who live and work in it. We are a BC landscaping company driven by a genuine passion for the craft, not just the contract.

Our team brings together experienced horticulturists, skilled tradespeople, and dedicated project managers who share a common standard. We listen first, plan carefully, and build things that last. Whether the project is a private residence, a large commercial site, or a strata complex, our commitment to quality does not change.

We take on work we know we can deliver exceptionally well. That selectiveness is what lets us put our name on every job with confidence.

Community

Good landscapes
build good places.

We serve a wide range of clients across the Lower Mainland and greater BC, from families wanting a backyard they can actually use, to developers who need a trusted landscaping partner from groundbreak to handover.

The spaces we build and maintain become part of neighbourhoods. They affect how people feel walking to their front door, how residents interact with shared outdoor areas, and how a business presents itself to the world. We take that seriously.

Building strong relationships with our clients and giving back to the communities we work in is not a side note. It is part of what drives us to keep doing this work well.

Why
Choose Us.

Real Expertise

Our team is made up of qualified horticulturists, experienced tradespeople, and project managers who have worked on some of the most demanding sites in the province. You get depth of knowledge, not just labour.

Every property is different. Before we propose anything, we take the time to understand your goals, your site, and your expectations. What we build is shaped by that conversation, not by a template.

Consistent Quality

We hold the same standard on a small residential job as we do on a large commercial development. Quality is not something we reserve for certain clients. It is the baseline we start from every time.

We plan our projects thoroughly so that timelines are realistic from the start. When we commit to a schedule, we keep it. Delays cost you money and trust, and we treat both with respect.

Transparent Process

No hidden costs, no surprises mid-project. We communicate clearly at every stage, and if something changes we tell you straight away. You always know where your project stands.

BC Focused

We know this province intimately. Our understanding of BC's varied climates, soil conditions, and plant communities means we design and build with the local environment in mind, not against it.
